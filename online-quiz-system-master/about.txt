Quizzer is an online quiz management system designed to conduct
secure and time based quizzes for students.

This platform allows users to register, login and attempt quizzes
based on different subjects and topics.

Each quiz is controlled by a timer to ensure fair evaluation
and automatic submission once the time is over.

Quizzer helps teachers and institutions to easily create quizzes,
manage questions and evaluate results efficiently.

Students can view their scores instantly after quiz completion
and track their performance.

This system is simple, fast and user friendly,
making online assessments easy and reliable.
